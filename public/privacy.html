Privacy Policy for CalcMind

Effective Date: May 2, 2026

Welcome to CalcMind. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application and services.

1. Information We Collect

When you use CalcMind, we may collect the following information:

  • Account Information: If you log in using Google Authentication, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture to create and manage your account.
  • Gameplay Data: We collect information about your quiz performance, scores, mistakes, and progress to update the leaderboard and improve your learning experience.
  • Device Information: We may collect non-personal information about your device, such as operating system and app version, for analytics and troubleshooting.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • To manage your account and display your profile on the leaderboard.
  • To track your progress and personalize your quiz experience (e.g., "My Mistakes" feature).
  • To analyze usage trends and fix technical issues.

3. Data Sharing and Security

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is securely stored using Firebase (a Google service) which employs industry-standard security measures to protect against unauthorized access.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. If you wish to delete your account or data, please contact us through our support channels.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or need support, please contact us via our Telegram Community.
